SYRACUSE UNIVERSITY WOMEN'S CLUB LACROSSE

The Syracuse University Women's Club Lacrosse team is a D1 club lacrosse team in the NEWLL conference. We play year round against teams like Northeastern, Cornell, Army, Penn State and more! We always welcome new girls to try out each semester, so don't be afraid to contact us or check out our website.
